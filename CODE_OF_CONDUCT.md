# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard for participation:

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Avoid offensive, discriminatory, or dismissive language.
- Focus feedback and discussion on the technical content, not the contributor.
- Assume good faith — most disagreements in a technical repository come from differing environments and experience, not bad intent.

Violations may result in removal of contributions or restriction from further participation in this repository.
